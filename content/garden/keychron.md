I recently decided to upgrade my keyboard and landed on the [Keychron Q11 QMK](https://www.keychron.com/products/keychron-q11-qmk-custom-mechanical-keyboard) with the Gateron G Pro Brown switches and so far I'm really liking it!

![keychron promo image](/garden/keychron.png)

I knew I wanted to stay with a split keyboard for ergonomics.

What I like:

* Configuration is web-based with the [Via app](https://www.usevia.app/).
* The led effect is nice but not too overwhelming. I prefer the effects that react to typing so it's not lit up unless actively typing.
* The nobs feel nice for volume control. I haven't found a great use the second knob though.
* I like the open key design which makes cleaning it a lot easier.

What could be better:

* The space buttons are bit smaller that my last keyboard and so I find I tend to press near the edge so I'm having to adjust a bit
* A dedicated "end" button would be nice
